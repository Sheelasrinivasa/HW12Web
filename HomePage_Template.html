Assignment - Weather and Citipy

Here, the flow is to find unique latitude and longitude coordinates. Then from Citipy, unique city names are identified. For the list of Cities on weather api, key attitubute
(temprature, humidity, wind speed and cloudiness) values are fetched. I also have a scatter plot to show a range of coordinates across multiple locations.

Limitations
